My Municipal Government Services

Online Help

The My Municipal Government Services application helps residents locate municipal facilities and get information about municipal services such as recycling and waste pickup schedules. To get service information, enter an address in the search box, click on your location on the map, or use your current location (button). Your location will be highlighted on the map and information about your services will be displayed

Address Search

  1. Enter an address in the search bar
  2. Press enter to search immediately or select a result from the autocomplete list.
Those using a mobile phone or older browsers will need to select the Search icon
to display the search field.

My Location Search

The user can select the Geolocation icon
to conduct a search based on their current location.

Selecting a Location on the Map

The user can navigate the map then click on his location.

Municipal Services and Facility Information

Depending on your location and the services, the following may be displayed after indicating your location:

  • The schedule for waste collection
  • Facility locations, hours of operation, and details.
  • Contact information and links to respective websites for each service and facility.
The facilities will be sorted in order of distance, nearest to furthest. Directions to a facility may be obtained by clicking on the Directions button
.

Displaying Service Areas & Facilities on the Map

To display service areas or facilities on the map, simply select the header for the service you wish to display. If facilities are displayed, you can obtain further details on a facility by selecting it on the map.

Map Functions

  • Use the basemap selector
    (not available on mobiles) to display your preferred basemap.
  • Select + or – in the upper left of the map to zoom in or out.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share icon
to share your map in an email, a Facebook post or on Twitter.

Browser Compatibility

The My Municipal Government Services app is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft Internet Explorer 7/8/9/10, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Apple Safari, iOS Safari 4.0+, Android Chrome 2.3+, and most modern JavaScript enabled browsers.
